About Us

Glenmarie Homestay was founded and is managed by folks who love to travel. It is set between magnificent tea gardens, with the Mighty Kancheunjungha Ranges as a backdrop. Glenmarie, which opened in 2014, has been consistently offering top-notch dining, accommodation, and tourism services to its guests.
